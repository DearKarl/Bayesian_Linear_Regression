# Route 2: Bayesian Risk and Decision Theory

This route studies task-dependent uncertainty, posterior risk, epistemic excess
risk, and the mathematical connection between predictive distributions and
actions. Its starting literature includes Wang and Holmes (AISTATS 2025) and
task-aware Bayesian-risk formulations for structured language-model outputs.

Planned reproductions will distinguish predictive uncertainty from the expected
loss of an action and test whether risk defined in a task-relevant latent space
aligns better with output quality than language-space entropy alone.

**Status:** literature protocol defined; no experiment has yet been claimed as
reproduced.
